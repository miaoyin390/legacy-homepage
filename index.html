Biography

Mr. Miao Yin is a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering (advised by Prof. Bo Yuan) at Rutgers University. He fortunately interned at Google (Mentor: Yilin Wang, Qifei Wang and Boqing Gong), building the efficient inference framework for YouTube video quality assessment.

Mr. Miao Yin's research mainly focuses on algorithm-hardware co-design for efficient AI systems (particularly DNN model optimization/compression for computer vision tasks on resource-constrained devices) based on higher-order tensor decomposition and advanced optimization. His research interests include:

  • On-device computer vision
  • Low-rank tensor decomposition
  • Efficient inference/training
  • AutoML
